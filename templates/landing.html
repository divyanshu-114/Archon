WHAT IS ARCHON

Not Just Another AI Tool.
An Entire Agency.

ARCHON is a multi-agent AI system built with LangGraph. Unlike ChatGPT or other LLMs where you prompt, copy, and paste — ARCHON's agents work together autonomously. They research your industry, craft platform-optimized content, quality-check it, schedule optimal posting times, and publish directly to your channels. You just approve.

🧠

Multi-Agent Intelligence

6 specialized AI agents collaborate through a state machine — each one an expert in their domain. Not one model doing everything; a team doing it right.

🔄

Self-Correcting

The Critic agent reviews all content and autonomously rejects anything below quality standards. Content gets rewritten until it's perfect — no human needed.

⚡

End-to-End Execution

From task to published post in under 30 seconds. Researches your market, writes content, reviews quality, schedules timing, and publishes — all automatically.

🛡️

Human-in-the-Loop

Nothing goes live without your approval. Review every message, approve what you love, reject or regenerate what you don't. You stay in control.
